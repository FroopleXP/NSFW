  SwapMouseButtons Instructions

 Swap the mouse buttons using the keyboard

  • Press Ctrl-F12 once.

  • A small window will appear in the center of the screen to indicate the main mouse button assignment.

                     

 Swap the mouse buttons using the Quick Launch Icon

  • Click on the SwapMouseButtons icon in the Quick Launch toolbar (make sure you use the currently active mouse button).

                     

  • A small window will appear in the center of the screen to indicate the main mouse button assignment.

                     

 How to change the key assignment

  1. Press Ctrl-F12 to activate SwapMouseButtons.

  2. Click inside the small window that appears. An "Options..." button will appear.

                     

  3. Press the "Options..." button. A dialog box will appear. Click on the "Hot key" tab.

  4. In the "Hot key" edit box, press the key combination you wish to assign to SwapMouseButtons. Then press the "Set Hot Key" button.

  5. Close the dialog box and exit the SwapMouseButtons program.

              

 How to disable pointer flipping

  1. Press Ctrl-F12 to activate SwapMouseButtons.

  2. Click inside the small window that appears. An "Options..." button will appear.

                     

  3. Press the "Options..." button. A dialog box will appear. Click on the "Pointers" tab.

  4. Click on the "Flip arrow and other mouse pointers" checkbox to remove the checkmark.

  5. Close the dialog box and exit the SwapMouseButtons program.

              

 Notes and known problems

  • Some non-Microsoft mouse drivers may interfere with the operation of the SwapMouseButtons program. For example, this may occur with trackpad drivers on some laptop computers.

  • Animated mouse pointers are not supported yet in this version of SwapMouseButtons. When the pointers are flipped to left-handed mode, the animation will become disabled.

  • In some rare cases when using Remote Desktop, the flipping of the mouse pointers to left-handed pointers may cause the pointer image to become distorted upon logging in remotely. To restore the pointer, press Ctrl-F12 twice.

  • When assigning a hot key, to avoid conflicts with other software, assign a seldom used key combination such as Ctrl-F11 or Ctrl-F12.

  • In older versions of Windows such as Windows 98, it is possible to assign keys such as F11 or F12 without the Ctrl key.

  • After changing the hot key, the previous hot key will become disabled after logging off or restarting Windows.

 How to uninstall

  1. Click on the Start menu and select "Settings" > "Control Panel".

  2. Double click on "Add or Remove Programs".

  3. Find "SwapMouseButtons" click on it.

  4. Click on the "Change/Remove" button.

  5. Follow the uninstallation sequence that continues.
